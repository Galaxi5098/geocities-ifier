GeoCities-ifier — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

Overview

GeoCities-ifier is a Chrome extension that applies retro 1990s-era visual styling to webpages. Your privacy is important, and this extension is designed to collect as little data as possible.

Data Collection

GeoCities-ifier does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The extension does not use analytics, tracking, cookies, or any form of telemetry.

Website Content

The extension reads and modifies the visual presentation of webpages in your browser tab when you activate it. All modifications happen locally in your browser. No page content is sent to any external server.

Permissions

The extension requests the following permissions:

  • activeTab — To access the current tab's content when you click the extension icon.
  • scripting — To inject the CSS and JavaScript that applies the retro visual effects.

These permissions are used solely to apply visual transformations to the active tab. No data is read, logged, or transmitted.

Third Parties

This extension does not share data with any third parties. There are no ads, analytics services, or external data processors.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date.
